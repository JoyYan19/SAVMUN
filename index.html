Delegate Procedures & Rules

Rules of Procedure

Standard MUN procedure with motions, points, and voting. Always ask the chair before speaking.

Dress Code

Business formal mandatory. No jeans, shorts, or casual footwear. Non-compliance may restrict entry.

Attendance

Attendance is mandatory. Three unexcused absences can disqualify from awards.

Speaker Etiquette

Direct remarks to the chair. Respect others and maintain diplomatic language.

FAQ

What if I arrive late?

Check in with the chair; frequent lateness can affect award eligibility.

Can I use electronics?

Electronics are not allowed during the conference.

How are awards chosen?

Based on diplomacy, research, participation, speech, and position paper quality.
